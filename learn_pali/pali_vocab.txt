Pali Vocabulary
Chapter 1
Nouns:
manussa - man, human being
brāhmana - brahmin
kumāra - boy
nara/purisa - man, person
putta - son
vānija - merchant
kassaka - farmer
mātula - uncle
bhūpāla - king
Buddha/Tahāgata/Sugata - the Buddha
sahāya/sahāyaka/mitta - friend

Verbs:
bhāsati - speaks
bhuñjati - eats
chindati - cuts
dhāvati - runs
pacati - cooks
sayati - sleeps
gacchati - goes
kasati - ploughs
passati - sees
āgacchati - comes

Exercise 1:
1. Bhūpālo bhuñjati. 
The king eats.
2. Puttā sayanti 
The sons sleep.
3. Vāṇijā sayanti 
The merchants sleep.
4. Buddho passati 
The Buddha sees.
5. Kumāro dhāvati. 
The boy runs.
6. Mātulo kasati. 
The uncle ploughs.
7. Brāhmaṇā bhāsanti. 
The brahmins speak.
8. Mittā gacchanti. 
The friends go.
9. Kassakā pacanti. 
The farmers cook.
10. Manusso chindati. 
The man/human being cuts.
11. Purisā dhāvanti. 
The people run.
12. Sahāyako bhuñjati. 
The friend eats.
13. Tathāgato bhāsati. 
The Buddha speaks.
14. Naro pacati. 
The man cooks. 
15. Sahāyā kasanti. 
The friends plow.
16. Sugato āgacchati.
The Buddha comes.
